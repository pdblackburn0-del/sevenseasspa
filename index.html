Wellness Tips

  • 1. Prioritize sleep — aim for 7–9 hours per night to support recovery and mood.
  • 2. Move daily — even a 20-minute walk improves circulation and reduces stress.
  • 3. Hydrate regularly — carry a water bottle and sip throughout the day.
  • 4. Practice mindful breathing — short breathing exercises calm the nervous system.
  • 5. Eat balanced meals — include protein, healthy fats, and whole grains for steady energy.
  • 6. Schedule downtime — unplug and do something restorative each day.
